About

Neonix Labs is independent from Neonix.AI. It is a public, open-source organization.

Neonix Labs shares Dr. Fei-Fei Li's vision of human-centered AI — that there is nothing truly "artificial" about AI: it is made by people, and so it must be guided by human concerns, augmenting human capability rather than replacing it, and respecting human dignity. To that, Neonix Labs adds a philosophy of open-source and decentralized governance.

Neonix Labs was founded for something that is hard to put into words — and for a future. If you share that future, get in touch: Labs@neonix.ai.
